ভালো প্রশ্ন 👍 **Subject** বুঝে গেলে English speaking অনেক easy হয়ে যায়।

---

# 🟢 📘 Subject কী?

👉 **Sentence e je kaj kore বা jake niye kotha bola hocche = Subject**

👉 Example:

* **I** play football.
* **He** eats rice.

(I, He = Subject)

---

# 🔥 Subject কী কী হতে পারে (Full Details)

## 🔹 1. Pronoun (সবচেয়ে common)

👉 I, You, He, She, It, We, They

* I am a student.
* He is playing.
* They are happy.

---

## 🔹 2. Noun (মানুষ/জিনিস/স্থান)

👉 Person:

* Rahim is my friend.
* The teacher is kind.

👉 Thing:

* The book is interesting.
* The car is fast.

👉 Place:

* Dhaka is a big city.
* The park is সুন্দর

---

## 🔹 3. Proper Noun (নির্দিষ্ট নাম)

* Rahim plays football.
* Dhaka is crowded.
* Bangladesh is beautiful.

---

## 🔹 4. Common Noun

* A boy is running.
* The girl is singing.
* A dog is barking.

---

## 🔹 5. Gerund (Verb + ing as subject)

👉 Verb + ing = Subject

* **Swimming** is fun.
* **Reading** is good.
* **Playing** helps fitness.

---

## 🔹 6. Infinitive (to + verb)

* **To learn English** is important.
* **To eat healthy food** is good.

---

## 🔹 7. Clause (Full sentence as subject)

* **What he said** is true.
* **That she came late** is surprising.

---

## 🔹 8. There (Dummy Subject)

👉 Real subject pore ashe

* There is a book on the table.
* There are many students.

---

## 🔹 9. It (Dummy Subject)

👉 Weather / time / distance

* It is raining.
* It is 5 PM.
* It is far from here.

---

## 🔹 10. Compound Subject (2+ subject)

* Rahim and Karim are friends.
* He and I will go.

---

# 🎯 Easy Trick

👉 “Who?” / “What?” diye question korle subject ber hoy

Example:

* Rahim plays football
  👉 Who plays? → Rahim (Subject)

Great 👍 এটা English speaking এর **most important rule**—
👉 **Subject অনুযায়ী verb change হয় (Subject–Verb Agreement)**

আমি তোমাকে একদম clear করে দিচ্ছি 👇

---

# 🟢 📘 Main Rule (Easy)

👉 **Singular Subject → Singular Verb**
👉 **Plural Subject → Plural Verb**

---

# 🔹 1. I / You / We / They (Plural type)

👉 **Base verb (V1)** use হয় (no s/es)

* I play football
* You read books
* We go to school
* They eat rice

❌ ভুল: He play
✔ ঠিক: He plays

---

# 🔹 2. He / She / It (Singular)

👉 **Verb + s/es**

* He plays football
* She reads books
* It works well

---

# 🔹 3. “Be verb” (am / is / are)

👉 I → **am**
👉 He/She/It → **is**
👉 You/We/They → **are**

* I am happy
* He is tired
* They are playing

---

# 🔹 4. “Have” verb

👉 I/You/We/They → **have**
👉 He/She/It → **has**

* I have a pen
* He has a car

---

# 🔹 5. Singular vs Plural Noun

👉 Singular → verb + s/es
👉 Plural → base verb

* The boy plays
* The boys play

---

# 🔹 6. Compound Subject (and)

👉 2 ta subject → **plural verb**

* Rahim and Karim play
* He and I go

---

# 🔹 7. “Everyone / Someone / Nobody”

👉 **Always singular**

* Everyone likes music
* Someone is calling
* Nobody knows

---

# 🔹 8. “There is / There are”

👉 Singular → **is**
👉 Plural → **are**

* There is a book
* There are many books

---

# 🔹 9. Uncountable Noun (always singular)

👉 advice, water, rice, money

* Water is important
* Money is needed

---

# 🔹 10. Gerund (V+ing as subject)

👉 Always singular

* Swimming is fun
* Reading helps learning

---

# 🔥 SUPER SHORT TRICK

👉 He/She/It → **verb + s/es**
👉 Others → **normal verb**
