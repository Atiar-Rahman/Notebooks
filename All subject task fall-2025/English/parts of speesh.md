**Parts of Speech** are the basic building blocks of English grammar.  
There are **8 main parts of speech**, each with its own function in a sentence.

---

### 🧩 **1. Noun (naming word)**

**Definition:** A word that names a person, place, thing, or idea.  
**Examples:**

- _Person:_ **Rahim**, teacher
    
- _Place:_ **Dhaka**, school
    
- _Thing:_ **book**, car
    
- _Idea:_ **honesty**, love
    

**Example sentence:**  
👉 **`Rahim`** reads a **book** in the **library**.

---

### 🗣️ **2. Pronoun (replaces noun)**

**Definition:** A word that replaces a noun to avoid repetition.  
**Examples:** I, you, he, she, it, we, they, mine, ours

**Example sentence:**  
👉 **``Rahim`** is my friend. **He** is very kind.

---

### ⚙️ **3. Verb (action or state word)**

**Definition:** A word that shows an action or state of being.  
**Examples:** run, eat, is, are, have, write, go

**Example sentence:**  
👉 She **runs** every morning.  
👉 They **are** happy.

---

### 💫 **4. Adjective (describing word)**

**Definition:** A word that describes a noun or pronoun.  
**Examples:** beautiful, tall, red, honest, smart

**Example sentence:**  
👉 She is a **beautiful** girl.  
👉 The **tall** man is my uncle.

---

### 🎯 **5. Adverb (modifies verb, adjective, or another adverb)**

**Definition:** A word that tells **how, when, where, or how much** an action happens.  
**Examples:** quickly, very, always, here, well

**Example sentence:**  
👉 He runs **quickly**.  
👉 She is **very** smart.

---

### 🔗 **6. Preposition (shows relationship)**

**Definition:** A word that shows the relationship between a noun/pronoun and another word.  
**Examples:** in, on, at, under, over, between, with, to

**Example sentence:**  
👉 The book is **on** the table.  
👉 She sat **beside** him.

---

### 🧵 **7. Conjunction (joining word)**

**Definition:** A word that joins words, phrases, or sentences.  
**Examples:** and, but, or, because, although, while

**Example sentence:**  
👉 I like tea **and** coffee.  
👉 He came **because** it was important.

---

### 😃 **8. Interjection (expresses feeling)**

**Definition:** A word or phrase that shows sudden emotion or feeling.  
**Examples:** oh!, wow!, alas!, hooray!, ouch!

**Example sentence:**  
👉 **Wow!** What a beautiful view!  
👉 **Alas!** He is dead.

---

### 🧠 **Summary Table:**

| Part of Speech | Function                        | Example                       |     |
| -------------- | ------------------------------- | ----------------------------- | --- |
| Noun           | Names a person/place/thing      | **Dog**, **city**             |     |
| Pronoun        | Replaces noun                   | **He**, **they**              |     |
| Verb           | Shows action or state           | **Run**, **is**               |     |
| Adjective      | Describes noun                  | **Tall**, **happy**           |     |
| Adverb         | Describes verb/adjective/adverb | **Quickly**, **very**         |     |
| Preposition    | Shows relationship              | **In**, **on**, **under**     |     |
| Conjunction    | Joins words or clauses          | **And**, **but**, **because** |     |
| Interjection   | Expresses emotion               | **Wow!**, **Oops!**           |     |
