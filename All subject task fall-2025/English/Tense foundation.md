# 1. Tense কী?

**Tense** হলো এমন একটি Grammar Rule যা বলে—

> **কখন কোনো কাজ হচ্ছে, হয়েছে, বা হবে।**

### উদাহরণ

আমি ভাত খাই।

> I eat rice.

আমি ভাত খাচ্ছি।

> I am eating rice.

আমি ভাত খেয়েছি।

> I have eaten rice.

আমি ভাত খাব।

> I will eat rice.

দেখো, কাজ একই (eat), কিন্তু সময় বদলেছে। তাই Verb-ও বদলেছে।

---

# 2. একটি Sentence-এর Structure

ইংরেজি বাক্যের সবচেয়ে সাধারণ গঠন:

> **Subject + Verb + Object**

উদাহরণ:

**I play football.**

* Subject = I
* Verb = play
* Object = football

---

আরেকটি:

**She drinks tea.**

* Subject = She
* Verb = drinks
* Object = tea

---

# 3. Subject কী?

যে ব্যক্তি বা বস্তু কাজ করে, তাকে Subject বলে।

| Subject | বাংলা      |
| ------- | ---------- |
| I       | আমি        |
| We      | আমরা       |
| You     | তুমি/আপনি  |
| He      | সে (ছেলে)  |
| She     | সে (মেয়ে) |
| It      | এটি        |
| They    | তারা       |

---

# 4. Verb কী?

যে শব্দ কাজ বোঝায়।

Examples

* eat
* go
* play
* read
* write
* speak
* study

---

# 5. Object কী?

যার উপর কাজটি হয়।

উদাহরণ:

I eat **rice**.

Object = rice

She reads **a book**.

Object = a book

# 6. Verb Forms (সবচেয়ে গুরুত্বপূর্ণ)

| V1    | V2    | V3      | বাংলা    |
| ----- | ----- | ------- | -------- |
| eat   | ate   | eaten   | খাওয়া   |
| go    | went  | gone    | যাওয়া   |
| write | wrote | written | লেখা     |
| see   | saw   | seen    | দেখা     |
| do    | did   | done    | করা      |
| make  | made  | made    | তৈরি করা |
| come  | came  | come    | আসা      |
| take  | took  | taken   | নেওয়া   |
| speak | spoke | spoken  | কথা বলা  |
| give  | gave  | given   | দেওয়া   |

### মনে রাখার নিয়ম

* **V1** = বর্তমান (Base Form)
* **V2** = অতীত
* **V3** = Perfect Tense-এ ব্যবহৃত হয়

# 7. Helping Verb

Helping Verb মূল Verb-কে সাহায্য করে।

### সবচেয়ে ব্যবহৃত Helping Verb

| Helping Verb | ব্যবহার       |
| ------------ | ------------- |
| am           | I-এর সাথে     |
| is           | He, She, It   |
| are          | You, We, They |
| was          | Past          |
| were         | Past          |
| have         | Perfect       |
| has          | Perfect       |
| had          | Past Perfect  |
| will         | Future        |

---

উদাহরণ

I **am** reading.

* Helping Verb = am
* Main Verb = reading

She **has** finished.

* Helping Verb = has
* Main Verb = finished

উদাহরণ

Main Verb = playing

Helping Verb = is

# কখন **-s** যোগ হবে?

|---|---|

# কখন **-es** যোগ হবে?

যদি verb-এর শেষে নিচের যেকোনো অক্ষর বা ধ্বনি থাকে, তাহলে **-es** যোগ হয়।

| --------- | --------------------- |
| pass      | passes                |

| miss      | misses                |

**Examples**

- He **passes** the exam.
    
- She **kisses** her baby.
    

---

### 2. -sh দিয়ে শেষ হলে

---

### 4. -x দিয়ে শেষ হলে

| Base Verb | Third Person Singular |
| --------- | --------------------- |
| fix       | fixes                 |
| mix       | mixes                 |

- He **fixes** computers.

### 5. -z দিয়ে শেষ হলে

## সহজে মনে রাখার ট্রিক

**Verb-এর শেষে যদি থাকে:**

> **S, SH, CH, X, Z, O → ES**

অর্থাৎ:

- pass → passes
    
- wash → washes
    
- watch → watches
    
- fix → fixes
    
- buzz → buzzes
    
- go → goes
    
- do → does
    

**Consonant + Y → IES**

- study → studies
    
- try → tries
    
- carry → carries
    

**বাকি সব Verb → S**

- play → plays
    
- read → reads
    
- eat → eats
    
- work → works
    
- write → writes
    

এটাই Simple Present Tense-এ **Third Person Singular**-এর জন্য **s/es** যোগ করার মূল নিয়ম।

# 3. s/es যোগ করার নিয়ম

### Rule 1: সাধারণভাবে **s** যোগ হবে

# ১. Simple Present Tense (Negative)

## Structure

### I / We / You / They

**Subject + do not (don't) + V1 + Object**

### He / She / It / একবচন নাম

> **মনে রাখো:** **does** ব্যবহার করলে Main Verb-এ আর **s/es** থাকবে না।

---

## Examples

### I / We / You / They

* We do not watch TV.
* They don't eat meat.
* You do not study at night.

---

### He / She / It

* He does not play football.
* He doesn't play football.
* She does not read books.
* Rahim doesn't go to school on Friday.
* My father does not smoke.

---

# খুব গুরুত্বপূর্ণ Rule

❌ He doesn't **plays** football.

# ৩. WH Questions

WH Words হলো এমন প্রশ্নের শব্দ যেগুলো **Yes/No** দিয়ে উত্তর দেওয়া যায় না।

| WH Word | বাংলা  |
| ------- | ------ |
| What    | কী     |
| Where   | কোথায় |

| Why     | কেন    |
| Who     | কে     |
| Which   | কোনটি  |
| Whose   | কার    |
| Whom    | কাকে   |
| How     | কীভাবে |

---

How does she cook?

---

### Who

Who teaches you English?

Who lives here?

> **নোট:** যদি **Who** নিজেই Subject হয়, তাহলে **do/does** লাগবে না।

# ১. Present Perfect Tense কী?

**Present Perfect Tense** এমন একটি কাজ বোঝায়—

* যা **অতীতে শুরু হয়ে শেষ হয়েছে**, কিন্তু তার **প্রভাব এখনো আছে**।
* অথবা জীবনের কোনো অভিজ্ঞতা বোঝায়।
* অথবা কোনো কাজ **এইমাত্র** শেষ হয়েছে।

### উদাহরণ

* আমি কাজ শেষ করেছি।
  → **I have finished my work.**

* সে স্কুলে চলে গেছে।
  → **She has gone to school.**

---

# ২. Structure

## Positive

**Subject + has/have + V3 + Object**

### Have ব্যবহার হবে

* I
* We
* You
* They

### Has ব্যবহার হবে

* He
* She
* It
* Rahim
* My father

---

## Examples

* I have finished my homework.
* We have completed the project.
* They have arrived.
* He has eaten rice.
* She has written a letter.
* Rahim has gone home.

---

# ৩. V3 (Past Participle) মনে রাখো

| V1    | V2    | V3      |
| ----- | ----- | ------- |
| eat   | ate   | eaten   |
| go    | went  | gone    |
| write | wrote | written |
| do    | did   | done    |
| see   | saw   | seen    |
| speak | spoke | spoken  |
| take  | took  | taken   |
| come  | came  | come    |
| make  | made  | made    |
| read  | read  | read    |

---

# ৪. Negative Sentence

## Structure

**Subject + has/have + not + V3**

সংক্ষেপে:

* have not = **haven't**
* has not = **hasn't**

### Examples

* I have not finished my work.
* We haven't eaten yet.
* He has not come.
* She hasn't called me.
* Rahim hasn't gone to school.

---

# ৫. Interrogative Sentence

## Structure

**Has/Have + Subject + V3 + Object?**

### Examples

* Have you finished your homework?
* Have they arrived?
* Has he eaten?
* Has she written the email?
* Has Rahim gone home?

---

## উত্তর

**Question:**
Have you finished your work?

**Answer:**

* Yes, I have.
* No, I haven't.

---

# ৬. WH Questions

## Structure

**WH Word + has/have + Subject + V3?**

### Examples

* What have you done?
* Where has he gone?
* Why have they left?
* When has she arrived?
* How have you learned English?

---

# ৭. গুরুত্বপূর্ণ Keywords

## Just = এইমাত্র

* I have just finished my work.
* She has just arrived.

---

## Already = ইতিমধ্যে

* I have already eaten.
* They have already left.

---

## Yet = এখনও (সাধারণত Negative/Question)

* I haven't finished yet.
* Have you finished yet?

---

## Ever = কখনো

* Have you ever visited Cox's Bazar?
* Have you ever eaten sushi?

---

## Never = কখনোই না

* I have never smoked.
* She has never driven a car.

# ৮. Present Perfect vs Simple Past

| Present Perfect          | Simple Past                   |
| ------------------------ | ----------------------------- |
| I have finished my work. | I finished my work yesterday. |
| সময় উল্লেখ নেই          | নির্দিষ্ট সময় উল্লেখ আছে     |
| ফলাফল এখনো গুরুত্বপূর্ণ  | অতীতের ঘটনা                   |

### উদাহরণ

# ১. Present Perfect Continuous Tense কী?

**Present Perfect Continuous Tense** এমন একটি কাজ বোঝায়—

* অতীতে শুরু হয়েছে,
* **এখনও চলছে**,
* এবং কতক্ষণ ধরে চলছে তা বোঝানো হয়।

### উদাহরণ

আমি দুই ঘণ্টা ধরে পড়ছি।

➡️ **I have been studying for two hours.**

সে সকাল থেকে কাজ করছে।

➡️ **She has been working since morning.**

---

# ২. Structure

## Positive

**Subject + has/have + been + V1 + ing + since/for**

### Have been

* I
* We
* You
* They

### Has been

* He
* She
* It
* Rahim

---

## Examples

* I have been reading for two hours.
* We have been working since morning.
* They have been playing since 4 PM.
* He has been sleeping for three hours.
* She has been cooking since 8 o'clock.

---

# ৩. Since এবং For

এটি এই Tense-এর সবচেয়ে গুরুত্বপূর্ণ অংশ।

## Since = নির্দিষ্ট সময় (Starting Point)

| Since         | উদাহরণ        |
| ------------- | ------------- |
| since 2020    | since 2020    |
| since Monday  | since Monday  |
| since morning | since morning |
| since 8 AM    | since 8 AM    |
| since January | since January |

### Examples

* I have been living here **since 2022**.
* She has been studying **since morning**.
* He has been waiting **since 9 AM**.

---

## For = সময়ের দৈর্ঘ্য (Duration)

| For          | উদাহরণ        |
| ------------ | ------------- |
| for 2 hours  | দুই ঘণ্টা ধরে |
| for 5 days   | পাঁচ দিন ধরে  |
| for 3 months | তিন মাস ধরে   |
| for 10 years | দশ বছর ধরে    |

### Examples

* I have been studying **for three hours**.
* They have been playing **for one hour**.
* We have been working **for five years**.

---

## সহজে মনে রাখো

* **Since = কখন থেকে**
* **For = কতক্ষণ ধরে**

# ১. Simple Past Tense কী?

# ১. Simple Past Negative

## Structure

> ⭐ **সবচেয়ে গুরুত্বপূর্ণ নিয়ম:**

# ২. কেন V1 হবে?

দেখো:

* He **went** to school. ✅ (V2)

* He **didn't go** to school. ✅ (V1)

কারণ **did** ইতিমধ্যেই Past বোঝাচ্ছে।

# ১. Past Perfect Tense কী?

পরে: left → **left**

# ১. Simple Future Tense কী?

**Simple Future Tense** এমন কাজ বোঝায় যা **ভবিষ্যতে ঘটবে**।

### উদাহরণ

* আমি আগামীকাল স্কুলে যাব।
  → **I will go to school tomorrow.**

* সে তোমাকে সাহায্য করবে।
  → **She will help you.**

---

# ২. Structure

## Positive Sentence

**Subject + will + V1 + Object**

> ⭐ **সব Subject-এর জন্য "will" একই থাকবে।**

| Subject | Helping Verb | Main Verb |
| ------- | ------------ | --------- |
| I       | will         | V1        |
| We      | will         | V1        |
| You     | will         | V1        |
| They    | will         | V1        |
| He      | will         | V1        |
| She     | will         | V1        |
| It      | will         | V1        |

---

## Examples

* I will study English.
* We will play football.
* They will visit Dhaka.
* He will buy a laptop.
* She will write a letter.
* Rahim will help me.

---

# ৩. কখন ব্যবহার হয়?

## (১) ভবিষ্যতের পরিকল্পনা বা সিদ্ধান্ত

* I will call you tonight.
* She will join the meeting tomorrow.

---

## (২) প্রতিশ্রুতি (Promise)

* I will always help you.
* We will never forget you.

---

## (৩) ভবিষ্যৎ সম্পর্কে অনুমান (Prediction)

* It will rain tomorrow.
* Bangladesh will win the match.

---

## (৪) হঠাৎ নেওয়া সিদ্ধান্ত (Instant Decision)

* The phone is ringing.
* **I will answer it.**

# ৫. Negative Sentence

## Structure

**Subject + will not (won't) + V1 + Object**

সংক্ষেপে:

* will not = **won't**

### Examples

* I will not go.
* I won't be late.
* She won't come.
* They won't play.
* We won't forget.

---

# ৬. Interrogative Sentence

## Structure

**Will + Subject + V1 + Object?**

### Examples

* Will you come tomorrow?
* Will she help me?
* Will they join us?
* Will Rahim study English?

---

## উত্তর

**Question:**

Will you come tomorrow?

**Answer:**

* Yes, I will.
* No, I won't.

---

# ৭. WH Questions

## Structure

**WH Word + will + Subject + V1 + Object?**

### Examples

* What will you do tomorrow?
* Where will she go?
* When will they arrive?
* Why will he leave?
* How will you solve the problem?

---

# ৮. Future Time Expressions

* soon
* later
* tonight
* in the future

### Examples

* I will visit my grandparents **next week**.
* She will call me **tonight**.

# ৯. Common Mistakes

✅ He will **go** to school.

---

---

✅ They will **play** football.

✅ I will **go** there.

**Future Continuous Tense** এমন একটি কাজ বোঝায় যা **ভবিষ্যতের কোনো নির্দিষ্ট সময়ে চলতে থাকবে**।

### উদাহরণ

আগামীকাল রাত ৮টায় আমি পড়াশোনা করব (চলমান থাকবে)।

➡️ **I will be studying at 8 PM tomorrow.**

সে তখন রান্না করবে (রান্না চলবে)।

➡️ **She will be cooking at that time.**

---

# ২. Structure

## Positive

**Subject + will be + V1 + ing + Object**

> ⭐ সব Subject-এর জন্য **will be** একই থাকবে।

### Examples

* I will be studying.
* We will be playing football.
* They will be watching TV.
* He will be sleeping.
* She will be cooking dinner.

---

# ৩. কখন ব্যবহার হয়?

## (১) ভবিষ্যতের একটি নির্দিষ্ট সময়ে কাজ চলবে

* I will be studying at 9 PM.
* She will be working tomorrow morning.
* They will be traveling next week.

---

## (২) ভবিষ্যতের দুটি কাজের মধ্যে একটি চলছিল

* I will be studying **when** you arrive.
* She will be cooking **when** we reach home.

---

## (৩) ভবিষ্যতের পরিকল্পিত চলমান কাজ

* This time tomorrow, we will be flying to Dubai.
* Next Monday, I will be attending a conference.

---

# ৫. Negative Sentence

## Structure

**Subject + will not be + V1 + ing**

সংক্ষেপে:

**will not = won't**

### Examples

* I won't be sleeping.
* She won't be working.
* They won't be waiting.
* We won't be playing.
* Rahim won't be studying.

---

# ৬. Interrogative Sentence

## Structure

**Will + Subject + be + V1 + ing?**

### Examples

* Will you be studying tonight?
* Will she be working tomorrow?
* Will they be waiting?
* Will Rahim be sleeping?

---

## উত্তর

**Question:**

Will you be studying tonight?

**Answer:**

* Yes, I will.
* No, I won't.

---

# ৭. WH Questions

## Structure

**WH Word + will + Subject + be + V1 + ing?**

### Examples

* What will you be doing tomorrow?
* Where will she be staying?
* When will they be arriving?
* Why will he be leaving?
* How will you be traveling?

### উদাহরণ

✅ I will call you tomorrow.

(আমি আগামীকাল ফোন করব।)

(আগামীকাল রাত ৮টায় আমি ফোন করার মধ্যে থাকব।)

# ১. Future Perfect Tense কী?

**Future Perfect Tense** এমন একটি কাজ বোঝায় যা **ভবিষ্যতের একটি নির্দিষ্ট সময়ের আগে শেষ হয়ে যাবে।**

👉 অর্থাৎ ভবিষ্যতে একটি নির্দিষ্ট সময় বা ঘটনার **আগেই** কাজটি সম্পন্ন হবে।

### উদাহরণ

* আমি আগামীকাল সন্ধ্যার আগে কাজ শেষ করে ফেলব।
  → **I will have finished the work before tomorrow evening.**

* সে রাত ১০টার মধ্যে বাড়ি পৌঁছে যাবে।
  → **She will have reached home by 10 PM.**

---

**Subject + will have + V3 + Object**

> ⭐ সব Subject-এর জন্য **will have** একই।

---

* We will have left the office.

**Will + Subject + have been + V1 + ing?**

# ১. Voice কী?

**Voice** বোঝায় একটি বাক্যে **কাজটি কে করছে** অথবা **কার ওপর কাজটি হচ্ছে**।

Voice দুই ধরনের:

1. **Active Voice**
2. **Passive Voice**

---

# ২. Active Voice কী?

যখন **Subject নিজে কাজটি করে**, তখন সেটি Active Voice।

### Structure

**Subject + Verb + Object**

### Examples

* Rahim writes a letter.
* I eat rice.
* She teaches English.
* They play football.
* We clean the room.

👉 এখানে Subject কাজ করছে।

---

# ৩. Passive Voice কী?

যখন **Object-এর ওপর কাজটি হয়**, তখন সেটি Passive Voice।

### Structure

**Object + be verb + V3 + by + Subject**

### Examples

* A letter is written by Rahim.
* Rice is eaten by me.
* English is taught by her.
* Football is played by them.
* The room is cleaned by us.

👉 এখানে গুরুত্ব Object-এর ওপর।

---

# ৪. Subject, Verb, Object কীভাবে চিনবে?

### Example 1

**Rahim writes a letter.**

* Subject = Rahim
* Verb = writes
* Object = a letter

---

### Example 2

**The teacher teaches English.**

* Subject = The teacher
* Verb = teaches
* Object = English

---

### Example 3

**They play cricket.**

* Subject = They
* Verb = play
* Object = cricket

---

# ৫. Active → Passive করার ধাপ

### Step 1

Object-কে সামনে আনো।

### Step 2

Tense অনুযায়ী **Be Verb** বসাও।

### Step 3

Main Verb-এর **V3** ব্যবহার করো।

### Step 4

প্রয়োজনে শেষে **by + Subject** বসাও।

---

## Example

### Active

Rahim writes a letter.

### Step 1

Object সামনে

A letter

### Step 2

Be Verb

is

### Step 3

V3

written

### Step 4

by Rahim

### Passive

**A letter is written by Rahim.**

# ৬. Pronoun Change

| Active | Passive (by-এর পরে) |
| ------ | ------------------- |
| I      | me                  |
| We     | us                  |
| You    | you                 |
| He     | him                 |
| She    | her                 |
| They   | them                |
| It     | it                  |

### Examples

* I write a letter.
  → A letter is written by **me**.

* She teaches us.
  → We are taught by **her**.

* They help him.
  → He is helped by **them**.

# ৭. Simple Present Active → Passive

## Formula

### Active

**Subject + V1(s/es) + Object**

### Passive

# 📌 ১. Double Object কী?

কিছু Verb-এর পরে **দুটি Object** থাকে।

যেমন:

* I gave **him** **a book**.
* She sent **me** **a letter**.
* The teacher taught **us** **English**.

এখানে দুটি Object আছে:

* **Indirect Object (ব্যক্তি)** → him, me, us
* **Direct Object (বস্তু)** → book, letter, English

# 📌 ২. Double Object-এর Passive

দুটি উপায়ে Passive করা যায়।

### Active

### Passive (Person-কে Subject)

I was given a gift by her.

### Passive (Thing-কে Subject)

A gift was given to me by her.

---

### আরও উদাহরণ

**Active:**

The teacher taught us English.

**Passive 1:**

We were taught English by the teacher.

**Passive 2:**

English was taught to us by the teacher.

---

**Active:**

My father bought me a bike.

**Passive 1:**

**Passive 2:**

A bike was bought for me by my father.

> 💡 **to** বা **for** ব্যবহার Verb-এর উপর নির্ভর করে।

> * give → to
> * send → to
> * teach → to
> * buy → for
> * make → for

# 📌 ৬. Pronoun Change Rule

Pronoun পরিবর্তন করার সবচেয়ে সহজ নিয়ম:

### Rule 1: First Person → Subject অনুযায়ী

| Direct | Indirect         |
| ------ | ---------------- |
| I      | Subject অনুযায়ী |
| We     | Subject অনুযায়ী |
| My     | Subject অনুযায়ী |
| Our    | Subject অনুযায়ী |

### Example

> "I am tired."

➡️ Rahim said that **he** was tired.

---

### Rule 2: Second Person → Object অনুযায়ী

| Direct | Indirect        |
| ------ | --------------- |
| You    | Object অনুযায়ী |
| Your   | Object অনুযায়ী |

### Example

He told me,

> "You are honest."

➡️ He told me that **I** was honest.

### Rule 3: Third Person অপরিবর্তিত থাকে

| Direct | Indirect |
| ------ | -------- |
| He     | He       |
| She    | She      |
| They   | They     |
| It     | It       |

# 📌 ৭. Tense Change Rule (Basic)

| can                | could                   |
| may                | might                   |

# 📌 ১. Assertive Sentence কী?

যে বাক্যে কোনো তথ্য, মতামত বা বক্তব্য প্রকাশ করা হয় তাকে **Assertive Sentence** বলে।

### উদাহরণ

* He said, **"I am busy."**
* She said, **"I like tea."**
* Rahim said, **"I will help you."**

---

# 📌 ২. Assertive Sentence-এর Formula

### Direct Speech

**Subject + Reporting Verb + "Reported Speech"**

⬇

### Indirect Speech

**Subject + Reporting Verb + (that) + Reported Clause**

> **that** সাধারণত ব্যবহার করা হয়, তবে আধুনিক ইংরেজিতে অনেক সময় বাদও দেওয়া যায়।

---

# 📌 ৩. say, said, tell, told-এর পার্থক্য

| Verb | Object লাগে? | উদাহরণ                    |
| ---- | ------------ | ------------------------- |
| say  | ❌ না         | He said, "Hello."         |
| said | ❌ না         | She said, "Good morning." |
| tell | ✅ হ্যাঁ      | He tells me the truth.    |
| told | ✅ হ্যাঁ      | She told me the news.     |

---

## Rule

### যদি Object না থাকে

**said** ব্যবহার হবে।

✅ He said, "I am happy."

---

### যদি Object থাকে

**told** ব্যবহার হবে।

✅ He told me that he was happy.

---

❌ He told that he was happy.

✅ He told **me** that he was happy.

---

# 📌 ৪. said → told

### Rule

**said to + Object = told + Object**

---

### Example 1

Direct:

He said to me,

> "I am busy."

Indirect:

He **told me** that he was busy.

---

### Example 2

Direct:

She said to Rahim,

> "You are honest."

Indirect:

She **told Rahim** that he was honest.

---

# 📌 ৫. Pronoun Change (Advanced)

## First Person → Reporting Subject অনুযায়ী

### Example

Rahim said,

> "I am ready."

➡️ Rahim said that **he** was ready.

---

## Second Person → Reporting Object অনুযায়ী

### Example

He said to me,

> "You are honest."

➡️ He told me that **I** was honest.

---

### আরেকটি উদাহরণ

Mother said to the children,

> "You are good."

➡️ Mother told the children that **they** were good.

---

## Third Person → পরিবর্তন হয় না

### Example

He said,

> "She is my friend."

➡️ He said that she was his friend.

---

# 📌 ৬. Tense Change (Revision)

| Direct             | Indirect                |
| ------------------ | ----------------------- |
| Simple Present     | Simple Past             |
| Present Continuous | Past Continuous         |
| Present Perfect    | Past Perfect            |
| Simple Past        | Past Perfect            |
| Past Continuous    | Past Perfect Continuous |
| will               | would                   |
| shall              | should / would*         |
| can                | could                   |
| may                | might                   |

* **Shall** অনেক ক্ষেত্রে **would** (১ম পুরুষ) বা **should**-এ পরিবর্তিত হতে পারে, প্রসঙ্গ অনুযায়ী।

---

### Examples

**Direct:**

He said,

> "I write letters."

**Indirect:**

He said that he wrote letters.

---

**Direct:**

She said,

> "I am studying."

**Indirect:**

She said that she was studying.

---

**Direct:**

They said,

> "We have completed the work."

**Indirect:**

They said that they had completed the work.

---

**Direct:**

Rahim said,

> "I will come."

**Indirect:**

Rahim said that he would come.

# 📌 ৩. দুই ধরনের Question

* When

* Whose
* Whom
* How

# 📌 ৪. Yes / No Question-এর Formula

# 📌 ১. Imperative Sentence কী?

যে বাক্যে **আদেশ, অনুরোধ, উপদেশ, নিষেধ, নির্দেশ বা অনুরোধ** করা হয়, তাকে **Imperative Sentence** বলে।

### উদাহরণ

* Open the door.
* Please help me.
* Don't run.
* Study regularly.
* Never tell a lie.

---

# 📌 ২. Imperative Sentence-এর Formula

### Direct

**Reporting Verb + "Imperative Sentence"**

⬇

### Indirect

**Reporting Verb + Object + to + Verb**

---

## উদাহরণ

### Direct

He said to me,

> "Open the door."

### Indirect

He **ordered** me **to open** the door.

---

# 📌 ৩. Reporting Verb নির্বাচন

| Direct Sentence | Indirect Reporting Verb |
| --------------- | ----------------------- |
| Order           | ordered                 |
| Command         | commanded               |
| Request         | requested               |
| Advice          | advised                 |
| Suggestion      | suggested*              |
| Warning         | warned                  |
| Urge            | urged                   |
| Permission      | allowed / permitted     |
| Prohibition     | forbade                 |

> **দ্রষ্টব্য:** **suggest** সাধারণত **to + verb** নেয় না। এর বিশেষ নিয়ম আছে, যা Day 27-এ বিস্তারিত শিখব।

### Indirect

The officer **ordered** the soldier **to stand up.**

# 📌 ৫. Request (অনুরোধ)

যদি **please**, kindly ইত্যাদি থাকে, সাধারণত **requested** ব্যবহার করা হয়।

### Indirect

He **requested** me **to help him.**

---

আরও উদাহরণ

* She said to me, "Please wait here."
  → She requested me to wait there.

* Mother said to Rahim, "Please come early."
  → Mother requested Rahim to come early.

---

# 📌 ৬. Advice (উপদেশ)

### Indirect

# 📌 ৮. Prohibition (নিষেধ)

### Direct

The teacher said,

> "Do not enter the lab."

# 📌 ১. Exclamatory Sentence কী?

যে বাক্যে **আনন্দ, দুঃখ, বিস্ময়, রাগ, প্রশংসা, আফসোস** ইত্যাদি আবেগ প্রকাশ করা হয় তাকে **Exclamatory Sentence** বলে।

শেষে সাধারণত **! (Exclamation Mark)** থাকে।

### উদাহরণ

* Hurrah! We have won the match!
* Alas! He is dead!
* Bravo! You did well!
* What a beautiful flower!
* How nice the weather is!

---

# 📌 ২. Exclamatory Sentence-এর Formula

### Direct

**Reporting Verb + "Exclamatory Sentence!"**

⬇

### Indirect

**Subject + Reporting Verb + that + Statement**

> তবে Reporting Verb আবেগ অনুযায়ী পরিবর্তন হবে।

---

| ---------- | ------------------------- |

| Sorrow     | exclaimed with sorrow     |
| Surprise   | exclaimed with surprise   |
| Wonder     | exclaimed with wonder     |
| Admiration | exclaimed with admiration |
| Pain       | exclaimed with pain       |
| Regret     | exclaimed with regret     |

# 📌 ৪. Hurrah!

### Direct

> "Hurrah! We have won the match!"

### Indirect

He **exclaimed with joy** that they had won the match.

---

* Hurrah! Bangladesh won the game!
  → He exclaimed with joy that Bangladesh had won the game.

---

> "Alas! My grandfather is dead!"

### Indirect

She **exclaimed with sorrow** that her grandfather was dead.

---

আরও উদাহরণ

* Alas! We have lost everything!
  → They exclaimed with sorrow that they had lost everything.

---

# 📌 ৬. Bravo!

### Direct

The teacher said,

> "Bravo! You have done well!"

### Indirect

The teacher **exclaimed with admiration** that I had done well.

---

# 📌 ৭. Oh!

### Direct

He said,

> "Oh! I forgot the key!"

### Indirect

He **exclaimed with surprise** that he had forgotten the key.

---

# 📌 ৮. What / How দিয়ে Exclamatory

### Direct

She said,

> "What a beautiful flower!"

### Indirect

---

### Direct

He said,

> "How wonderful the place is!"

### Indirect

He **exclaimed with admiration** that the place was very wonderful.

---

# 📌 ৯. Optative Sentence কী?

যে বাক্যে **Wish, Prayer, Blessing, Curse** প্রকাশ করা হয় তাকে **Optative Sentence** বলে।

### উদাহরণ

* May you live long!
* May Allah bless you!
* Long live our country!
* May God help us!

---

# 📌 ১০. Optative Sentence-এর Formula

### Direct

May + Subject + Verb...

⬇

### Indirect

Subject + **wished / prayed** + that...

---

# 📌 ১১. Wish

### Direct

Father said,

> "May you succeed in life!"

### Indirect

Father **wished** that I might succeed in life.

---

আরও উদাহরণ

* May you be happy!
  → He wished that I might be happy.

---

# 📌 ১২. Prayer

### Direct

The Imam said,

> "May Allah bless you!"

### Indirect

The Imam **prayed** that Allah might bless me.

---

### আরও উদাহরণ

* May God help us!
  → He prayed that God might help them.

---

# 📌 ১৩. Curse

### Direct

He said,

> "May you suffer forever!"

### Indirect

He **cursed** that I might suffer forever.

---

# 📌 ১৪. Long Live

### Direct

They said,

> "Long live Bangladesh!"

### Indirect

They **wished** that Bangladesh might live long.

# 📌 ৩. Pronoun Change (Revision)

### First Person

👉 Reporting Subject অনুযায়ী পরিবর্তন হবে।

**Example**

> "I am ready."

---

### Second Person

👉 Reporting Object অনুযায়ী পরিবর্তন হবে।

**Example**

He said to me,

> "You are honest."

➡ He told me that **I** was honest.

# 📌 ১. Transformation of Sentences কী?

**অর্থ (Meaning) পরিবর্তন না করে বাক্যের গঠন (Structure) পরিবর্তন করাকে Transformation of Sentences বলে।**

### Example

**Assertive:**

> He is honest.

**Negative:**

> He is not dishonest.

অর্থ একই, কিন্তু গঠন ভিন্ন।

---

# 📌 ২. Assertive → Negative

### Rule

যদি বাক্যটি Positive হয়, তাহলে Negative করতে হবে এমনভাবে যেন **অর্থ একই থাকে**।

---

### Example 1

**Assertive:**
He is honest.

**Negative:**
He is **not dishonest**.

---

### Example 2

**Assertive:**
Asif is always punctual.

**Negative:**
Asif is **never late**.

---

### Example 3

**Assertive:**
Everyone loves peace.

**Negative:**
**Nobody** hates peace.

---

### আরও উদাহরণ

| Assertive            | Negative                     |
| -------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| He is brave.         | He is not cowardly.          |
| She is kind.         | She is not unkind.           |
| This is possible.    | This is not impossible.      |
| Everybody knows him. | Nobody is unaware of him.    |
| He always helps me.  | He never refuses to help me. |

---

# 📌 ৩. Negative → Assertive

### Example

**Negative:**
He is not weak.

**Assertive:**
He is strong.

---

**Negative:**
It is not impossible.

**Assertive:**
It is possible.

---

# 📌 ৪. Assertive → Interrogative

### Rule

Positive Statement-কে এমন প্রশ্নে রূপান্তর করতে হবে যার উত্তর **Yes** হবে।

---

### Example

**Assertive:**
He is honest.

**Interrogative:**
**Isn't he honest?**

---

**Assertive:**
She sings well.

**Interrogative:**
**Doesn't she sing well?**

---

**Assertive:**
They can swim.

**Interrogative:**
**Can't they swim?**

---

### আরও উদাহরণ

| Assertive            | Interrogative              |
| -------------------- | -------------------------- |
| He works hard.       | Doesn't he work hard?      |
| She helps everyone.  | Doesn't she help everyone? |
| They are friends.    | Aren't they friends?       |
| You know the answer. | Don't you know the answer? |
| He has finished.     | Hasn't he finished?        |

---

# 📌 ৫. Interrogative → Assertive

### Example

**Interrogative:**
Isn't he honest?

**Assertive:**
He is honest.

---

**Interrogative:**
Don't you know me?

**Assertive:**
You know me.

---

# 📌 ৬. Exclamatory → Assertive

### Rule

**What** বা **How** বাদ দিয়ে **very**, **great**, **extremely** ইত্যাদি ব্যবহার করে Statement বানানো হয়।

---

### Example

**Exclamatory:**
What a beautiful flower!

**Assertive:**
It is a **very beautiful** flower.

---

**Exclamatory:**
How nice the weather is!

**Assertive:**
The weather is **very nice**.

---

### আরও উদাহরণ

| Exclamatory                 | Assertive                    |
| --------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| What a brave boy!           | He is a very brave boy.      |
| How sweet she is!           | She is very sweet.           |
| What a wonderful idea!      | It is a very wonderful idea. |
| How beautiful the river is! | The river is very beautiful. |
| What an exciting game!      | It is a very exciting game.  |

# 📌 ৭. Degree of Comparison

ইংরেজিতে তিন ধরনের Degree আছে।

| Degree      | Example                                |
| ----------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Positive    | Karim is as tall as Rahim.             |
| Comparative | Karim is taller than Rahim.            |
| Superlative | Karim is the tallest boy in the class. |

---

# 📌 ৮. Positive → Comparative

### Rule

**as...as** → **-er than**

---

### Example

**Positive:**
Rahim is as tall as Karim.

**Comparative:**
Rahim is **not taller than** Karim.

---

**Positive:**
This book is as useful as that one.

**Comparative:**
That book is **not more useful than** this one.

---

# 📌 ৯. Comparative → Superlative

### Example

**Comparative:**
Rahim is taller than any other boy in the class.

**Superlative:**
Rahim is **the tallest** boy in the class.

---

**Comparative:**
Iron is more useful than any other metal.

**Superlative:**
Iron is **the most useful** metal.

---

# 📌 ১০. Superlative → Comparative

### Example

**Superlative:**
Mount Everest is the highest mountain in the world.

**Comparative:**
Mount Everest is **higher than any other** mountain in the world.

---

## Narration Formula

### Assertive

said → that

### Question

asked → if / whether / WH-word

### Command

ordered/requested/advised + to + Verb

### Exclamatory

exclaimed with joy/sorrow/surprise/admiration

### Optative

wished / prayed + that

---

## Transformation Formula

| Type                      | Formula                       |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Assertive → Negative      | Opposite Word                 |
| Assertive → Question      | Negative Question             |
| Exclamatory → Assertive   | very + adjective              |
| Comparative → Superlative | any other → the + superlative |

# 🎯 25 Most Important Grammar Rules

1. Subject–Verb Agreement ঠিক রাখতে হবে।
2. Tense-এর Structure মুখস্থ নয়, বুঝে শিখতে হবে।
3. V1, V2, V3-এর ব্যবহার জানতে হবে।
4. Modal-এর পরে সবসময় V1।
5. Passive Voice-এ সবসময় V3।
6. Narration-এ Pronoun পরিবর্তন করতে হবে।
7. Reporting Verb Past হলে সাধারণত Tense এক ধাপ Past-এ যায়।
8. Universal Truth-এর Tense পরিবর্তন হয় না।
9. Question Sentence-এ **asked** ব্যবহার হয়।
10. Imperative-এ **to + Verb**।
11. Negative Imperative-এ **not to + Verb**।
12. Exclamatory-তে Emotion অনুযায়ী Reporting Verb।
13. Article (a, an, the) সঠিকভাবে ব্যবহার করতে হবে।
14. Countable ও Uncountable Noun আলাদা করতে হবে।
15. Comparative-এর পরে **than**।
16. Superlative-এর আগে **the**।
17. Preposition মুখস্থ নয়, ব্যবহার করে শিখতে হবে।
18. One of + Plural Noun।
19. Each/Every + Singular Noun।
20. Either/Neither + Singular Verb।
21. Many + Plural Noun।
22. Much + Uncountable Noun।
23. Since (Point of Time), For (Period of Time)।
24. By + Time = কাজ শেষ হওয়ার সময়সীমা।
25. Never অনর্থক Double Negative ব্যবহার করবে না।
