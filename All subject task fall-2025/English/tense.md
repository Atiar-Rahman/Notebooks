
# 🟢 📘 Tense Full Chart (Simple Guide)

## 🔹 1. Present Tense (বর্তমান সময়)

### 1. Present Indefinite

👉 Habit / Routine / General truth  
**Structure:** Subject + V1 (+s/es)

- I play football.
    
- He goes to school.
    

---

### 2. Present Continuous

👉 এখন চলছে এমন কাজ  
**Structure:** Subject + am/is/are + V-ing

- I am eating.
    
- He is reading.
    

---

### 3. Present Perfect

👉 কাজ শেষ কিন্তু result present e ache  
**Structure:** Subject + have/has + V3

- I have eaten.
    
- She has finished her work.
    

---

### 4. Present Perfect Continuous

👉 long time dhore cholte thaka  
**Structure:** Subject + have/has been + V-ing

- I have been studying for 2 hours.
    

---

## 🔵 2. Past Tense (গত সময়)

### 1. Past Indefinite

👉 past e kisu hoye geche  
**Structure:** Subject + V2

- I went to school.
    
- He played football.
    

---

### 2. Past Continuous

👉 past e cholchilo  
**Structure:** was/were + V-ing

- I was reading.
    
- They were playing.
    

---

### 3. Past Perfect

👉 ekta past action er age onno ekta sesh hoise  
**Structure:** had + V3

- I had eaten before he came.
    

---

### 4. Past Perfect Continuous

👉 past e long time cholchilo  
**Structure:** had been + V-ing

- I had been studying for 3 hours.
    

---

## 🟠 3. Future Tense (ভবিষ্যৎ সময়)

### 1. Future Indefinite

👉 future e hobe  
**Structure:** will + V1

- I will go.
    
- She will eat.
    

---

### 2. Future Continuous

👉 future e cholte thakbe  
**Structure:** will be + V-ing

- I will be studying.
    

---

### 3. Future Perfect

👉 future e ekta kaj sesh hoye jabe  
**Structure:** will have + V3

- I will have finished my work.
    

---

### 4. Future Perfect Continuous

👉 future e long time cholte thakbe  
**Structure:** will have been + V-ing

- I will have been studying for 2 hours.
    

---

# 🔥 SUPER EASY TIP

👉 Present = now  
👉 Past = before  
👉 Future = after
