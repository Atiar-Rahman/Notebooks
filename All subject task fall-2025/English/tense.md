
# 🟢 📘 Tense Full Chart (Simple Guide)

## 🔹 1. Present Tense (বর্তমান সময়)

### 1. Present Indefinite

👉 Habit / Routine / General truth  
**Structure:** Subject + V1 (+s/es)

- I play football.
    
- He goes to school.
    

---

### 2. Present Continuous

👉 এখন চলছে এমন কাজ  
**Structure:** Subject + am/is/are + V-ing

- I am eating.
    
- He is reading.
    

---

### 3. Present Perfect

👉 কাজ শেষ কিন্তু result present e ache  
**Structure:** Subject + have/has + V3

- I have eaten.
    
- She has finished her work.
    

---

### 4. Present Perfect Continuous

👉 long time dhore cholte thaka  
**Structure:** Subject + have/has been + V-ing

- I have been studying for 2 hours.
    

---

## 🔵 2. Past Tense (গত সময়)

### 1. Past Indefinite

👉 past e kisu hoye geche  
**Structure:** Subject + V2

- I went to school.
    
- He played football.
    

---

### 2. Past Continuous

👉 past e cholchilo  
**Structure:** was/were + V-ing

- I was reading.
    
- They were playing.
    

---

### 3. Past Perfect

👉 ekta past action er age onno ekta sesh hoise  
**Structure:** had + V3

- I had eaten before he came.
    

---

### 4. Past Perfect Continuous

👉 past e long time cholchilo  
**Structure:** had been + V-ing

- I had been studying for 3 hours.
    

---

## 🟠 3. Future Tense (ভবিষ্যৎ সময়)

### 1. Future Indefinite

👉 future e hobe  
**Structure:** will + V1

- I will go.
    
- She will eat.
    

---

### 2. Future Continuous

👉 future e cholte thakbe  
**Structure:** will be + V-ing

- I will be studying.
    

---

### 3. Future Perfect

👉 future e ekta kaj sesh hoye jabe  
**Structure:** will have + V3

- I will have finished my work.
    

---

### 4. Future Perfect Continuous

👉 future e long time cholte thakbe  
**Structure:** will have been + V-ing

- I will have been studying for 2 hours.
    

---

# 🔥 SUPER EASY TIP

👉 Present = now  
👉 Past = before  
👉 Future = after

---

Tense হলো **কখন (Time)** কোনো কাজ হচ্ছে, হয়েছে, বা হবে—তা বোঝানোর জন্য verb-এর রূপ পরিবর্তন।

**মোট ১২টি Tense আছে।**

| Present (বর্তমান)          | Past (অতীত)             | Future (ভবিষ্যৎ)          |
| -------------------------- | ----------------------- | ------------------------- |
| Simple Present             | Simple Past             | Simple Future             |
| Present Continuous         | Past Continuous         | Future Continuous         |
| Present Perfect            | Past Perfect            | Future Perfect            |
| Present Perfect Continuous | Past Perfect Continuous | Future Perfect Continuous |

---

# ১. Simple Present Tense

**কাজ:** অভ্যাস, সত্য, নিয়মিত কাজ।

**Structure**

* Subject + V1(s/es) + Object

**Examples**

* I play football.
* She plays football.
* They study English.

**বাংলা**

* আমি ফুটবল খেলি।
* সে ফুটবল খেলে।

---

# ২. Present Continuous Tense

**কাজ:** এখন কাজ চলছে।

**Structure**

* Subject + am/is/are + V1 + ing

**Examples**

* I am reading.
* She is cooking.
* They are playing.

---

# ৩. Present Perfect Tense

**কাজ:** কাজ শেষ হয়েছে কিন্তু তার প্রভাব এখন আছে।

**Structure**

* Subject + has/have + V3

**Examples**

* I have finished my work.
* She has gone home.

---

# ৪. Present Perfect Continuous Tense

**কাজ:** অতীতে শুরু হয়েছে, এখনও চলছে।

**Structure**

* Subject + has/have + been + V1 + ing + since/for

**Examples**

* I have been studying for two hours.
* She has been working since morning.

---

# ৫. Simple Past Tense

**কাজ:** অতীতে কাজ শেষ হয়েছে।

**Structure**

* Subject + V2

**Examples**

* I went to school.
* She cooked rice.

---

# ৬. Past Continuous Tense

**কাজ:** অতীতে কোনো সময় কাজ চলছিল।

**Structure**

* Subject + was/were + V1 + ing

**Examples**

* I was reading.
* They were playing.

---

# ৭. Past Perfect Tense

**কাজ:** অতীতে একটি কাজ আরেকটি কাজের আগে শেষ হয়েছিল।

**Structure**

* Subject + had + V3

**Examples**

* I had finished before he came.
* She had left.

---

# ৮. Past Perfect Continuous Tense

**কাজ:** অতীতে একটি কাজ কিছু সময় ধরে চলছিল।

**Structure**

* Subject + had + been + V1 + ing + since/for

**Examples**

* I had been studying for two hours before dinner.

---

# ৯. Simple Future Tense

**কাজ:** ভবিষ্যতে হবে।

**Structure**

* Subject + will + V1

**Examples**

* I will go.
* She will help me.

---

# ১০. Future Continuous Tense

**কাজ:** ভবিষ্যতের নির্দিষ্ট সময়ে কাজ চলবে।

**Structure**

* Subject + will be + V1 + ing

**Examples**

* I will be studying.
* They will be playing.

---

# ১১. Future Perfect Tense

**কাজ:** ভবিষ্যতের নির্দিষ্ট সময়ের আগে কাজ শেষ হবে।

**Structure**

* Subject + will have + V3

**Examples**

* I will have finished my work by 8 PM.

---

# ১২. Future Perfect Continuous Tense

**কাজ:** ভবিষ্যতে একটি কাজ নির্দিষ্ট সময় পর্যন্ত চলতে থাকবে।

**Structure**

* Subject + will have been + V1 + ing + since/for

**Examples**

* I will have been working here for five years by next January.

| Every day, always, usually | Simple Present     |

| Yesterday, last year, ago  | Simple Past        |
| While, when                | Past Continuous    |
| Before, after              | Past Perfect       |
| Tomorrow, next week        | Simple Future      |
| By tomorrow                | Future Perfect     |
