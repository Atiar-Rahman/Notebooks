
## **1. Definition of Adjective**

An **adjective** is a word that **describes, qualifies, or modifies a noun or pronoun**.  
It tells us about **qualities, quantity, size, shape, color, feelings, etc.**

**Example:**

- _The **beautiful** flower is blooming._  
    → “beautiful” describes the noun “flower.”
    

---

## **2. Types of Adjectives**

### **A. Descriptive Adjectives**

Describe **qualities or features** of a noun.  
**Examples:**

- _She has a **bright** smile._
    
- _This is a **tall** building._
    

---

### **B. Quantitative Adjectives**

Tell **how much or how many**.  
**Examples:**

- _I have **three** apples._
    
- _There is **some** water in the glass._
    

---

### **C. Demonstrative Adjectives**

Point out **specific nouns**.  
**Examples:**

- _**This** book is interesting._
    
- _**Those** cars are expensive._
    

---

### **D. Possessive Adjectives**

Show **ownership or possession**.  
**Examples:**

- _This is **my** pen._
    
- _She loves **her** dog._
    

---

### **E. Interrogative Adjectives**

Used in **questions** to describe nouns.  
**Examples:**

- _**Which** dress do you like?_
    
- _**What** color is your car?_
    

---

### **F. Comparative and Superlative Adjectives**

Used to **compare nouns**.

- **Comparative:** compares **two things**
    
    - _She is **taller** than her brother._
        
- **Superlative:** compares **more than two things**
    
    - _He is the **smartest** student in the class._
        

---

### **G. Proper Adjectives**

Derived from **proper nouns** (names of places, people, etc.)  
**Examples:**

- _I love **Indian** food._
    
- _He reads **Shakespearean** literature._
    

---

### **H. Distributive Adjectives**

Refer to **individual members** of a group.  
**Examples:**

- _Every student must attend the class._
    
- _Each player gets a prize._
    

---

### **I. Articles (Adjectives)**

The words **a, an, the** are considered **adjectives** because they define nouns.  
**Examples:**

- _I saw **a** cat._
    
- _She wants **the** book on the table._
    

---

## **3. Position of Adjectives**

1. **Before the noun:**
    
    - _A **red** apple_
        
2. **After the verb (linking verbs):**
    
    - _The apple is **red**._
