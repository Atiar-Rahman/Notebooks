# ✅ 🌟 Daily English Speaking Routine (Repeat Everyday)

## 🔹 Step 1: Warm-up (5 min)

Easy sentence diye start koro

👉 Bolo:

- “Good morning”
    
- “Today I will practice English”
    
- “I am feeling good”
    

🎯 Goal: Brain ke English mode e ana

---

## 🔹 Step 2: Speaking Practice (10 min)

Mirror er samne darao & ekta topic niye bolo

👉 Day-wise topic:

- Day 1: Myself
    
- Day 2: My daily routine
    
- Day 3: My goal
    
- Day 4: My family
    
- Day 5: My favorite food
    
- Day 6: My hobby
    
- Day 7: Free topic
    

Tarpor abar repeat cycle 🔁

---

## 🔹 Step 3: Thinking Practice (5 min)

Saradin jeta koro, seta English e chinta koro

👉 Example:

- “I am going to eat”
    
- “I need to study”
    
- “I am feeling sleepy”
    

---

## 🔹 Step 4: Listening (10–15 min)

- YouTube / movie dekho English e
    
- Subtitle use korte paro
    

🎯 Goal: natural sentence shona

---

## 🔹 Step 5: Shadowing (5–10 min)

- Ekta video theke 2–3 line copy kore bolo
    
- Same speed, same tone try koro
    

🔥 Eta fluency fastest baray

---

## 🔹 Step 6: Vocabulary (5 min)

- 5 ta new word shikho
    
- Sentence e use koro
    

👉 Example:

- Happy → “I am happy today”
    

---

## 🔹 Step 7: Self Recording (5 min)

- Mobile diye voice record koro
    
- Nijer mistake identify koro
    

---

# 🧠 Golden Rules

✔ Bangla theke translate kora bondho  
✔ Wrong holeo bolo (fear remove koro)  
✔ Daily korte hobe (skip kora jabe na)

---

# 🔁 Total Time: 30–40 min per day

👉 Eta 30 din follow korle:

- Hesitation kome jabe
    
- Sentence flow asbe
    
- Confidence bere jabe
    

👉 60–90 din:

- Tumi easily English e kotha bolte parba 💯
