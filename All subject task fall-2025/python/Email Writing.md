
# 📧 Email Writing Basics (A to Z)

## 1. Email Structure (গঠন)

একটা standard email সাধারণত ৫টা অংশে হয়:

### ✅ 1. Subject (বিষয়)

➡️ Email কিসের সম্পর্কে — short & clear

**Example:**

* Request for Leave
* Job Application
* Meeting Schedule

---

### ✅ 2. Greeting (শুভেচ্ছা)

Formal:

* Dear Sir/Madam
* Dear Mr. Rahman

Informal:

* Hi John
* Hello Friend

---

### ✅ 3. Opening Line

➡️ কেন লিখছো সেটা বলো

**Example:**

* I hope you are well.
* I am writing to inform you that...
* I would like to request...

---

### ✅ 4. Main Body

➡️ Main কথা (clear + short)

---

### ✅ 5. Closing + Signature

**Closing:**

* Regards
* Best regards
* Thank you

**Signature:**

* Your name

---

# ✉️ Example 1 (Formal Email)

## 📌 Topic: Leave Application

Dear Sir,

I hope you are well. I am writing to request leave for two days due to personal reasons.

I kindly ask for your approval.

Thank you.

Best regards,
Atiar Rahman

---

# ✉️ Example 2 (Informal Email)

## 📌 Topic: Friend ke message

Hi Rahim,

How are you? I hope you are doing well. I just wanted to check on you.

Let’s meet soon.

Take care,
Atiar

---

# 🔥 Important Tips

✔ Short sentence use করো
✔ Clear English লিখো
✔ Grammar simple রাখো
✔ Over smart word use করো না

# 💼 1. Job Application Email

👉 এটা সবচেয়ে important (real life + freelancing + job)

## 📌 Structure

* Subject: Job Application for [Position]
* Greeting
* Introduction (নিজেকে introduce)
* Skills / Experience
* Closing

## ✉️ Example:

Dear Sir/Madam,

I hope you are doing well. I am writing to apply for the position of Junior Developer.

I have basic knowledge of Python, Django, and React. I am very interested in learning and growing in this field.

I have attached my CV for your consideration.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Best regards,
Atiar Rahman

---

# 🏢 2. Professional Email (Office Use)

👉 Office, client, boss — everywhere use হয়

## 📌 Example: Meeting Request

Dear Mr. Rahman,

I hope you are doing well. I would like to request a meeting to discuss the project updates.

Please let me know a convenient time for you.

Thank you.

Best regards,
Atiar

---

# 🎓 3. IELTS Email Writing (Formal Style)

👉 Exam + professional writing skill

## 📌 Example: Complaint Email

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with the service I received at your store.

The staff behavior was unprofessional, and the service was very slow. I hope you will take necessary action to improve this issue.

I look forward to your response.

Yours faithfully,
Atiar Rahman

---

# 🔥 Pro Tips (Advanced Level)

✔ “I am writing to…” → formal শুরু
✔ “I would like to…” → polite request
✔ “Please let me know…” → professional tone
✔ “Looking forward to…” → strong closing

# ✅ Cover Letter কী?

Cover letter হলো তোমার **নিজের introduction + কেন তুমি job-এর জন্য perfect** — এটা CV-এর সাথে পাঠানো হয়।

👉 সহজভাবে:
**CV = তোমার data**
**Cover Letter = তোমার story + convincing power**

---

# ✅ Cover Letter Structure (Must Follow)

## 1️⃣ Header

```
Your Name  
Your Email  
Date  
Company Name  
```

---

## 2️⃣ Greeting

```
Dear Hiring Manager,
```

(বা যদি নাম জানো → Dear Mr. Rahman)

---

## 3️⃣ Opening (First Paragraph)

👉 এখানে বলবে:

* কোন job এর জন্য apply করছো
* কোথায় job দেখেছো

✔ Example:

```
I am writing to apply for the position of Web Developer at your company. I found this job on LinkedIn and I am very interested in this opportunity.
```

---

## 4️⃣ Body (Main Part)

👉 এখানে explain করবে:

* তোমার skill
* experience
* কেন তুমি best

✔ Example:

```
I have experience in Django, React, and REST API development. I have built several projects, including a scholarship management system. I am passionate about building scalable web applications.
```

---

## 5️⃣ Closing Paragraph

👉 এখানে বলবে:

* তুমি interested
* interview চাই

✔ Example:

```
I would love the opportunity to contribute to your team. I am available for an interview at your convenience.
```

---

## 6️⃣ Ending

```
Sincerely,  
Your Name
```

# ❌ Wrong Way (copy-paste)


👉 এটা 100 জন পাঠায় → client ignore করে 😑

# ✅ Right Way (JD customize)

ধরো job description এ লেখা:
👉 “Need Django API with JWT authentication and clean code”

✔ তুমি লিখবে:

```id="right1"
I saw that you need a Django API with JWT authentication. I have experience building secure APIs with DRF and token-based authentication.
```

👉 দেখো difference:

* Client এর language use করছো ✔
* বুঝিয়েছো তুমি বুঝেছো ✔

👉 Cover letter এ use করো

* Authentication
* Fast delivery

✔ You write:

```id="formula1"
You need a REST API with authentication and quick delivery. I have experience building fast and secure APIs using Django.
```
